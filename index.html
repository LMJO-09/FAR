// About The Project

Redefining Agriculture
With Intelligent Robotics

Farm Bot is an intelligent agricultural robot fully designed and built in Jordan by the students Abdulrahman Al-Maaytah and Haitham Al-Maaytah. It monitors crops, detects harmful plants, makes autonomous decisions, and can be controlled remotely — representing the perfect integration of modern technology with traditional farming.

// Core Capabilities

What Farm Bot Can Do

🌱 Growth Tracking

Monitors crops continuously, tracking growth stages and identifying plants ready for harvest.

🛡️ Disease Detection

Automatically detects harmful plants and diseases, removing them without damaging healthy crops.

🧠 AI Decision Making

Operates independently using AI to make accurate agricultural decisions based on real‑time data.

📡 Remote Monitoring

Fully controllable from anywhere, giving farmers complete oversight of their fields.

📊 Detailed Reports

Generates comprehensive reports on land and crop conditions for smarter planning.

♻️ Resource Optimization

Reduces waste of water, fertilizers, and time — for truly sustainable farming.
